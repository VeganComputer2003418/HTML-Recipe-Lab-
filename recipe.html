Italian Cobb Salad

+

By Jamie. The best salad that you'll ever have.

+

For the marinated chicken +

    +
  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts
    • +
  • 1 tsp oregano
    • +
  • 1 tsp thyme
    • +
  • 1 tsp Basil
    • +
  • 1 bay leaf
    • +
  • 1/2 tsp rosemary
    • +
  • 1/2 tsp sage
    • +
  • 1/2 tsp salt
    • +
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
    • +
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
    • +
  • 1/8 cup red wine vinegar
    • +
  • 1 lemon juiced
    • +
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
    • +
+ + Directions: + +
    +
  1. Combine all spices in a medium bowl.
    2. +
  2. Whisk in red wine vinegar, lemon juice, and olive oil.
    3. +
  3. Add minced garlic cloves.
    4. +
  4. Whisk to combine.
    5. +
  5. Place thawed chicken breasts in a non reactive dish or bowl (a ziplock bag works great, too!).
    6. +
  6. Our marinade over chicken, tossing to combine.
    7. +
  7. Cover with plastic wrap (unless using ziplock) and allow to marinate on the counter for no less than 30 minutes.
    8. +
  8. If you plan to marinate it longer than that, place in the refrigerator.
    9. +
  9. When chicken is ready, heat a grill pan over medium heat.
    10. +
  10. Cook chicken, turning once, until charred and cooked through, about 10 minutes total.
    11. +
  11. Remove from heat, cover with tin foil, and allow to rest until ready to cube for salad.
    12. +
+ +

For the salad +

    +
  • 2 cups green leaf lettuce
    • +
  • 1 cup romaine lettuce
    • +
  • 1/3 cup cherry tomatoes halved
    • +
  • 1/4 cup red onion
    • +
  • 2 pieces Prosciutto
    • +
  • 1/2 cup Marinated artichokes
    • +
  • 1/4 cup castelvatrano olives
    • +
  • 1/8 cup Pepperocini, sliced
    • +
  • 1/4 cup Grana Padano cheese (or parmesan) Cubed or grated
    • +
  • 1 egg hardboiled
    • +
+ + Directions: + +
    +
  1. Meanwhile, prepare the prosciutto bacon: Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
    2. +
  2. Place prosciutto on a baking rack set in a baking sheet.
    3. +
  3. This allows the prosciutto to not cook in it's grease.
    4. +
  4. Feel free to bake directly on baking sheet, but know that it will be greasier.
    5. +
  5. Bake prosciutto until crispy, about 15 minutes. With closely to ensure it doesn't burn.
    6. +
  6. While prosciutto bacon is cooking, prep other ingredients.
    7. +
  7. Wash, dry, and chop lettuces.
    8. +
  8. Chop red onion, cherry tomatoes, slice pepperocini, as well as hard-boiled egg.
    9. +
  9. Cube or grate Grana Padano.
    10. +
+ +

For the vinaigrette +

    +
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
    • +
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
    • +
  • 2 tsp dijon mustard
    • +
  • 1 tsp garlic minced
    • +
  • Dash Bragg's aminos
    • +
  • Salt & Pepper to taste
    • +
+ + Directions: + +
    +
  1. Make the vinaigrette: combine all ingredients besides olive oil in a small bowl.
    2. +
  2. Whisk to combine, slowly drizzling in olive oil.
    3. +
  3. Season with salt and pepper.
    4. +
  4. To assemble the salad, mix romaine and green leaf lettuces together and place on plate.
    5. +
  5. Cube cooled chicken and crumble prosciutto bacon.
    6. +
  6. Arrange ingredients in vertical rows on top of greens: chicken, bacon, hard boiled egg, artichokes, tomato, red onion, pepperocini, and Gran Padano.
    7. +
  7. Top with olives, and drizzle with dressing.
    8. +
  8. Enjoy!
    9. +
+
